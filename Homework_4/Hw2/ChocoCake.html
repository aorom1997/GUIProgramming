How to Make a Chocolate Chip Cake!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt flour
  • 1 1/3 cups sour cream
  • 2/3 cup butter softened
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees (325 if using a glass pan).
  2. Grease a 9 X 13 pan with cooking spray.
  3. In a large bowl combine all of the cake batter ingredients.
  4. Mix for 1 minute at low speed; mix 3 minutes at medium speed.
  5. Pour 1/2 of batter into greased pan.
  6. Generously sprinkle cinnamon and sugar over batter making sure to get the corners and then sprinkle with 1/2 bag of chocolate chips.
  7. Pour remaining cake batter on top and spread across bottom layer. Repeat with cinnamon and sugar and chocolate chips.
  8. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick comes out with only a few crumbs (donâ€™t overbake or cake will be dry!).
